# Raw Data: Hebrew Parsing

## Daniel 9:24 Parsing
shabuwim shibim nechtak al-ammekha veal-ir qodshekha
lekhalle happesha ulehatem chattatot ulekhapper avon
ulehabi tsedeq olamim velachtom chazon venavi velimeshoach qodesh qodashim

Key morphology:
- nechtak: Niphal Perfect 3ms of chathak (H2852) - be cut off/determined
- lekhalle: Piel Infinitive Construct of kalah (H3615) - to finish
- ulehatem: Hiphil Infinitive Construct of tamam (H8552) - to complete/make an end
- ulekhapper: Piel Infinitive Construct of kaphar (H3722) - to atone/reconcile
- ulehabi: Hiphil Infinitive Construct of bo (H935) - to bring in
- velachtom: Qal Infinitive Construct of chatham (H2856) - to seal
- velimeshoach: Qal Infinitive Construct of mashach (H4886) - to anoint

All six purposes use infinitive constructs with prefixed lamed (to): purpose clauses.

## Daniel 8:14 Parsing
venitsdaq qodesh
- nitsdaq: Niphal Perfect 3ms of tsadaq (H6663) - be vindicated/justified
- qodesh: Noun ms Abs of qodesh (H6944) - sanctuary/holiness

## Leviticus 16:21 Parsing
vehitvadah alav et-kol-avonot bnei yisrael
veet-kol-pishshehem lekhol-chattatam
Three sin-words confirmed:
- avonot: mp Construct of avon (H5771) - iniquities
- pishshehem: mp Abs+3mp of pesha (H6588) - their transgressions/rebellions
- chattatam: fp Abs+3mp of chattat (H2403) - their sins
