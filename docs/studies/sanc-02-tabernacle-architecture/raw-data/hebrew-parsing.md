**Key notes:**

- betokam (בְּתֹוכָם) -- "in their midst" -- preposition + noun construct + 3mp suffix

**Key notes:**

- qodesh haqqodashim (קֹדֶשׁ הַקֳּדָשִׁים) -- "holy of holies" -- superlative construct: holiness of holinesses
