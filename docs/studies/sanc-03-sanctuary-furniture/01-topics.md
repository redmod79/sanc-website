# Topics Research

## Question
What does each piece of sanctuary furniture typify? Trace each item from its OT description through to its NT fulfillment. How does each piece teach a distinct aspect of salvation?

## Topics

### ALTAR (Nave's Full Entry)

**Of Burnt Offerings (Bronze Altar)**
- Called BRAZEN ALTAR (EXO 39:39; 1KI 8:64), ALTAR OF GOD (PSA 43:4), ALTAR OF THE LORD (MAL 2:13)
- Pattern: EXO 27:1-8; Constructed by Bezaleel: EXO 38:1-7
- Location: EXO 40:6,29 (first piece encountered in the courtyard)
- Furniture: EXO 27:3-7; 38:3-7
- Horns: EXO 27:2; uses of horns: PSA 118:27
- Sanctified by anointing: EXO 29:36,37,44; 30:26-28; 40:10; LEV 8:10,11; NUM 7
- Sanctified everything that touched it: EXO 29:37; 30:29; MAT 23:18,19
- A place of refuge: EXO 21:14; 1KI 1:50; 2:28

**Of Incense (Golden Altar)**
- Called the GOLDEN ALTAR (EXO 39:38; NUM 4:11), ALTAR OF SWEET INCENSE (LEV 4:7), ALTAR BEFORE THE LORD (LEV 16:18)
- Pattern: EXO 30:1-5; Constructed: EXO 37:25-28
- Location: EXO 30:6; 40:5,26 (before the veil, in the Holy Place)
- Uses: EXO 30:7-10,26,27; 40:27; LEV 4:7,18; 8:15; 9:9; 16:12,18
- In Solomon's temple: 1KI 6:19,20; 7:48; 1CH 28:18
- Seen in John's vision: REV 8:3; 9:13

### CANDLESTICK (Lampstand) (Nave's Full Entry)
