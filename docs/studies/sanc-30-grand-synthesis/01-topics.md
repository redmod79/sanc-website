# Topics Research

## Question
Drawing on ALL previous sanctuary series studies (sanc-01 through sanc-29) and the christ-sin-bearer-as-high-priest study, synthesize: What is the sanctuary? Why does it matter? How does it illuminate salvation, prophecy, Revelation, and God's character? What confidence can future studies place in the sanctuary framework?

## Topics

### SANCTUARY
- EXO 25:8; HEB 9:2; LEV 19:30; NUM 18:5; HEB 8:2,5; EZK 11:16
- Covers the physical structure (mishkan/miqdash), its purpose (dwelling), and its heavenly antitype

### TABERNACLE
- EXO 25:8-9; 26:1-37; 27:1-8; 33:7; 38:21; 40:34-38; HEB 8:2,5; 9:1-12,24
- Construction details, pattern theology, glory-filling ratification

### ATONEMENT
- LEV 16:2-34; 17:11; HEB 9:7,12,22,28; ROM 3:24-26
- kaphar vocabulary, DOA ritual, blood ministry, hilasterion bridge

### PRIEST / HIGH PRIEST
- GEN 14:18; HEB 5:6,10; 7:1-21,27; 9:7,12,28
- Melchizedek typology, priestly sin-bearing (nasa/anaphero), intercessory ministry

### PLAN OF SALVATION
- Cross-referenced to JESUS MISSION OF; REDEMPTION; SALVATION
- Sanctuary as visual curriculum for salvation

### PROPHECY
- DAN 7:9,10; 9:24-27; REV 15:3; 16:7
- Sanctuary vocabulary in Daniel and Revelation prophetic frameworks

### JUDGMENT
- DAN 7:9,10; ECC 12:14; MAT 12:36; REV 20:12
- Pre-advent judgment, courtroom imagery, books opened

### TEMPLE
- 1KI 6-8; 2CH 3-5; EZK 40-48; HAG 2:7-9; REV 11:19; 15:5-8; 21:22
- Solomon's temple, Ezekiel's vision temple, heavenly temple in Revelation
