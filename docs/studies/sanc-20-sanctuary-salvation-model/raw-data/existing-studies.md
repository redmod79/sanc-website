# Existing Studies Referenced

This study (sanc-20) is the capstone of the sanctuary series (sanc-01 through sanc-19). Key findings from prior studies are documented in PROMPT.md and incorporated throughout the research.

## Core Architectural Studies
- **sanc-01 (Why a Sanctuary):** Sanctuary purpose = dwelling (Exo 25:8). Thread: Eden -> Tabernacle -> Temple -> Incarnation (John 1:14) -> Believers (1 Cor 3:16) -> New Jerusalem (Rev 21:3). Sanctuary is "God's master illustration of the plan of salvation."
- **sanc-02 (Tabernacle Architecture):** Three-zone structure with material gradient (bronze/silver/gold). Single eastern entrance reverses Eden's expulsion. East-to-west = approach to God. Silver sockets = redemption foundation. Heb 9:8 ("the Holy Ghost this signifying"). Heb 10:19-22 collapses entire journey.
- **sanc-03 (Sanctuary Furniture):** Each piece = aspect of Christ's work. Bronze altar = justification. Laver = regeneration (louo) + sanctification (nipto). Lampstand = Christ/Spirit/churches. Showbread = bread of life. Incense = prayer/intercession. Mercy seat = hilasterion. "Perpetual" principle across all.

- **sanc-17 (Atonement Vocabulary):** kaphar/kippur/kapporeth/kopher word family. LXX bridge kapporeth -> hilasterion confirmed (PMI 9.75).
- **sanc-18 (Blood Ministry):** Blood = life-currency. Lev 17:11 theological foundation.
