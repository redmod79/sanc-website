**From sanc-01/CONCLUSION.md (Why a Sanctuary):**

**From sanc-02/CONCLUSION.md (Tabernacle Architecture):**
- Three-zone structure: outer court (bronze, judgment), Holy Place (gold, ongoing ministry), Most Holy Place (pure gold, God's presence)
- Material gradient: bronze (judgment/cleansing) -> silver (redemption foundation) -> gold (divine glory)
- Single eastern entrance reverses Eden's expulsion (Gen 3:24); east-to-west movement = approach to God
- Silver sockets (Exo 38:25-27) = redemption money foundation; kaphar root links foundation (kopher) to destination (kapporeth)
- Heb 9:8: "The Holy Ghost this signifying" -- the structure IS the message
- Heb 10:19-22 collapses the entire architectural journey into a single christological statement
- The furniture sequence traces: gate -> altar (sacrifice) -> laver (washing) -> lampstand (light) -> showbread (sustenance) -> incense (prayer) -> veil (flesh of Christ) -> mercy seat (face-to-face)
- New Jerusalem is an expanded Most Holy Place (cubic dimensions, Rev 21:16; no temple, Rev 21:22)

**From sanc-03/CONCLUSION.md (Sanctuary Furniture):**
- Bronze altar = substitutionary death, justification (Heb 13:10; 9:12,22)

- Lampstand = Christ as light (John 8:12), Spirit's power (Zec 4:6), churches (Rev 1:20); lychnia (lampstand) vs lychnos (lamp) distinction
- Showbread = bread of life (John 6:35,48-51); twelve loaves = complete community before God
- Incense altar = prayer/intercession (Psa 141:2; Rev 5:8; 8:3-4; Heb 7:25); liminal position before the veil
- Mercy seat = Christ as hilasterion (Rom 3:25; Heb 9:5); LXX bridge kapporeth -> hilasterion (PMI 9.75)
- Three ark contents: law (requirement), manna (provision), rod (authorization) -- all fulfilled in Christ
- Every piece has a heavenly counterpart in Revelation (4:5-6; 5:6; 8:3; 11:19)
- The "perpetual" principle: light always burning, bread always present, incense always rising = Christ's unending ministry

**From sanc-06/CONCLUSION.md (Sin Enters Sanctuary):**
