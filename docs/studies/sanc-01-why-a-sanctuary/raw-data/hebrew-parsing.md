**CRITICAL CONNECTION:**
- הִתְהַלַּכְתִּי (hithallakhti) — Verb.Hithpael.Perf.1s of הלך (halakh) — "I will walk about"
- This is the **EXACT SAME** Hithpael stem as מִתְהַלֵּךְ (mithallekh) in Genesis 3:8!
- Gen 3:8: God **walking** (Hithpael Participle) in the garden
- Lev 26:12: "I will **walk** (Hithpael Perfect) among you"
- The tabernacle promise in Lev 26:11-12 explicitly echoes the Eden reality of Gen 3:8
- God's stated goal: to restore the Eden relationship where He walks among His people
