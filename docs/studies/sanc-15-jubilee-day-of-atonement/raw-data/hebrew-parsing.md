**Key notes on Jer 34:15-17:**
- דרור appears THREE TIMES in these two verses
- The ironic reversal: "You did not proclaim deror... so I proclaim deror FOR YOU — to the sword, pestilence, famine"
- God uses the exact same verb קרא + דרור in both the positive (v.15) and ironic (v.17) senses
- In v.17, God is the one who "proclaims liberty" — but it's liberty FROM His protection
