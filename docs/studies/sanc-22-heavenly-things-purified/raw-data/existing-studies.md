# Existing Studies Summary

## daniel-8-14-sanctuary-cleansed
- **Question:** What does Dan 8:14 mean by "cleansed"?
- **Key finding:** nitsdaq (Niphal of tsadaq, H6663) = vindicated, not cleansed
- **Vocabulary:** tsadaq is forensic/legal; Lev 16 uses taher (cleanse) and kaphar (atone), NOT tsadaq
- **Dan 9:24 connection:** Same tsadaq root appears as "everlasting righteousness" (tsedeq olamim)
- **Evening-morning:** Refers to daily sacrifice cycle
- **Job parallels:** Same courtroom vocabulary for heavenly vindication

## sanctuary-vindication-meaning
- **Question:** What does it mean to legally vindicate the sanctuary?
- **Key finding:** A heavenly court renders verdict answering Satan's accusations
- **Four accusations:** Against God's truth/law, sanctuary/plan, saints, God's character
- **Zechariah 3 model:** Satan accuses Joshua → Lord rebukes → filthy garments removed → vindicated
- **Daniel 7 process / Daniel 8:14 outcome:** Court sits + books opened → sanctuary vindicated
- **Evidence in books:** Lives examined to demonstrate reality of faith

## ezekiel-sanctuary-defilement
- **Question:** What does Ezekiel say about sanctuary defilement?
- **Key finding:** Multiple sin categories defile: idolatry, violence, child sacrifice, Sabbath desecration, sexual immorality, social injustice, priestly failure
- **Glory departure:** Three stages (Ezek 9-11)
- **Root cause:** "The LORD seeth us not" (practical atheism)
- **Shared vocab with Daniel:** shiqquts (H8251) for abominations
- **Restoration promise:** Ezek 36:25 sprinkling clean water; 36:26-27 new heart
- **Ezekiel=WHY, Daniel=WHEN**

## sanc-21-hebrews-heavenly-ministry
- **Question:** What does Heb 8-10 teach about Christ's heavenly ministry?
- **Key finding:** Once-for-all modifies sacrifice, NEVER intercession; present tenses describe ongoing ministry
- **Heb 9:23 "better sacrifices":** kreittoisin thysiais is dative PLURAL
- **Copy→original logic:** earthly needed purification → heavenly needs purification (Heb 9:23 "therefore/oun")
- **Three phases:** Inauguration, intercession, judgment/Day of Atonement
- **Revelation progression:** Holy Place imagery → Most Holy Place imagery
- **ta hagia ambiguity:** Same form for Holy Place, Most Holy Place, entire sanctuary
