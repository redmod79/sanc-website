# Existing Studies Summary

## daniel-8-14-sanctuary-cleansed
- nitsdaq = Niphal Perfect of tsadaq H6663 = vindicated, not cleansed
- Daniel chose forensic/legal term rather than ritual vocabulary
- Sanctuary (qodesh) is subject receiving vindication
- Evening-morning (erev boqer) lacks conjunction -- compound unit
- Job connection: tsadaq in courtroom drama
- Dan 7 judgment scene = same event as Dan 8:14 vindication
- Dan 9:24 same vocabulary: tsedeq olamim + qodesh qodashim

## daniel-8-14-evening-morning-vs-day-of-atonement
- Dan 8:14 evening-morning = grammatically unique construction
- Does NOT match DOA evening to evening (Lev 23:32)
- Parallels Creation pattern (Gen 1:5)
- Daniel avoided using yom
- DOA connection comes through CONTEXT not through evening-morning grammar

## sanctuary-vindication-meaning (referenced in PROMPT.md)
- Four dimensions: God s truth/law, God s people, God s plan, God s character
- Zechariah 3 model: Satan accuses -> God rebukes -> filthy garments removed
- Dan 8:12 truth (emeth) cast to ground = attack on God s law
- Dan 8:13 sanctuary trodden under foot = attack on God s plan

## sanc-19-vindication-courtroom
- ONLY Niphal occurrence of tsadaq in entire OT
- LXX translates with dikaioo (PMI 26.99) -- forensic meaning confirmed
- Four courtroom roles: Judge, Accuser, Advocate, Accused
- Isaiah 53:11 breaks zero-sum: righteous Servant justifies many
- Rom 3:25-26: God just AND justifier
- Rev 15:3, 16:5,7, 19:2 = three waves of vindication declaration

## sanc-24-daniel-7-court
- Dan 7:9-14 = formal heavenly court
- DOA parallels: white garments, fire, cloud, books, exclusion
- Dan 7:13 Son of Man moves TOWARD God (heavenly event)
- Three-phase judgment: pre-advent, millennial, post-millennial
- Dan 8:14 vindication = OUTCOME of Dan 7 judicial PROCESS
