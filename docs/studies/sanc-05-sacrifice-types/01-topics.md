# Topics Research

## Question
What are the major sacrifice types in the Levitical system (burnt, sin, trespass, peace, grain), what does each one accomplish theologically, and how does each find fulfillment in Christ?

## Topics

### OFFERINGS
**Source:** Nave's Topical Bible

**General regulations:**
- Offered at the door of the tabernacle (LEV 1:3; 3:2; 17:4,8,9) or temple (2CH 7:12; 1KI 8:62)
- All animal sacrifices must be eight days old or more (LEV 22:27)
- Must be salted (LEV 2:13; EZK 43:24; MRK 9:49)
- Some with leaven (LEV 7:13; AMO 4:5), some without (EXO 23:18; 34:25)

**BURNT (olah) -- LEV 9:2:**
- Purpose: to make atonement for sin (LEV 1:4; 7)
- Core ordinances: EXO 29:15-18; LEV 1; 5:7-10; 6:9-13; 17:8,9; 23:18,26-37; NUM 15:24,25; 19:9; 28:26-31; 29
- Accompanied by other offerings (NUM 15:3-16)
- Skins belonged to priests (LEV 7:8)
- Offered daily morning and evening: GEN 15:17; EXO 29:38-42; LEV 6:20; NUM 28; 29:6; 1CH 16:40; 2CH 2:4; 13:11; EZR 3:3; EZK 46:13-15
- Music with burnt offerings (NUM 10:10)

**MEAT/GRAIN (minchah):**
- Ordinances: EXO 29:40,41; 30:9; 40:29; LEV 2; 5:11,12; 6:14-23; 7:9-13,37; 9:17; 23:13,16,17; NUM 4:16; 5:15,18,25,26; 8:8; 15:3-16,24; 18:9; 28:5,9,12,13,20,21,26-31; 29:3,4,14
- Not mixed with leaven (LEV 2:4,11; 6:14-18; 10:12,13; NUM 6:15,17)
- Eaten in the holy place (LEV 10:13; NUM 18:9,10)

**PEACE (shelamim):**
- Laws: EXO 29:19-22,31; LEV 7:11-15,18; 9:3,4,15-21; 23:19; NUM 6:14; 10:10

**SIN (chattat):**
- Ordinances: EXO 29:10-14 (with HEB 13:11-13); LEV 4; 5; 6:1-7,26-30; 9:1-21; 12:6-8; 14:19,22,31; 15:30; 23:19; NUM 6:10,11,14,16; 8:8,12; 15:27; 28:15,22-24,30; 29:5,6,11,16-38
- Temporary: DAN 11:31; HEB 9; 10

**TRESPASS (asham):**
- Ordinances: LEV 5; 6:1-7; 7:1-7; 14:10-22; 15:15,29,30; 19:21,22; NUM 6:12; EZR 10:19
- Eaten by priests (LEV 7:6,7; 14:13; NUM 18:9,10)
- Even offered by idolaters (1SA 6:3,8,17,18)

**THANK (todah):**
- LEV 7:11-15; 22:29; DEU 12:11,12

**ANIMAL SACRIFICES AS TYPE OF CHRIST:**
- PSA 40:6-8 with HEB 10:1-14; ISA 53:11,12 with LEV 16:21; JHN 1:29; 1CO 5:7; 2CO 5:21; EPH 5:2; HEB 9:19-28; 10:1,11,12; 13:11-13; REV 5:6

**INSUFFICIENCY OF OFFERINGS:**
- HEB 8:7-13; 9:1-15; 10:1-12,18-20

**USELESS WITHOUT GENUINE PIETY:**
- 1SA 15:22; PSA 40:6; 50:8-14; 51:16,17; PRO 21:3,27; ISA 1:11-14; 40:16; 66:3; JER 6:20; 7:21-23; 14:12; HOS 6:6; 8:13; AMO 5:21-24; MIC 6:6-8; MRK 12:33

### ATONEMENT

**Day of Atonement:** EXO 30:10; LEV 16:2-34; 23:27-32; NUM 29:7-11; HEB 5:3; 9:7,19,22

**Made by Jesus:**

- Made but once: HEB 7:27; 9:24-28; 10:10,12,14; 1PE 3:18

### BLOOD
**Source:** Nave's Topical Bible

**Is the life:** GEN 9:4; LEV 17:11,14; DEU 12:23

**Sacrificial blood procedures by offering type:**
- Sin offering: sprinkled 7x before curtain (LEV 4:5,6,17); on horns of incense altar and bottom of burnt offering altar (EXO 30:10; LEV 4:7,18,25,30; 5:9; 9:9,12)
- Trespass offering: sprinkled on the altar (LEV 7:2)
- Burnt offering: sprinkled all around and upon the altar (EXO 29:16; LEV 1:5,11,15; 8:19; DEU 12:27)
- Peace offering: sprinkled about the altar (LEV 3:2,8,13; 9:18)
- Atonement: sprinkled on mercy seat (LEV 16:14,15,18,19,27; 17:11)

**Blood of Christ:** MAT 26:28; ROM 3:24,25; 5:9; 1CO 10:16; EPH 1:7; 2:13; COL 1:14,20; HEB 9:12-14; 10:19,20; 13:12; 1PE 1:18,19; 1JN 1:7; REV 1:5; 5:9; 7:14; 12:11
