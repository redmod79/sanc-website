## H3680 kasah (verb, 152 BLB occurrences)
**Definition:** a primitive root; properly, to plump, i.e. fill up hollows; by implication, to cover (for clothing or secrecy): clad self, close, clothe, conceal, cover (self), (flee to) hide, overwhelm
**Note:** Parallel covering concept to kaphar. Kasah = physical covering; kaphar = spiritual/ritual covering.
