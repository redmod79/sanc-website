**Pattern:** The triad of PRIEST + BLOOD + SIN forms the core of Hebrews' argument. The Levitical HP enters with animal blood for sins of ignorance (9:7); Christ enters with His own blood having put away sin (9:12,26). The repetition (yearly) is contrasted with the finality (once).
