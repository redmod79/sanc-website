# Topics Research

## Question
Conduct a comprehensive survey of all sanctuary vocabulary and imagery in Revelation. What sanctuary furniture, rituals, and terminology does John use, and where? How does Revelation systematically deploy the sanctuary framework?

## Topics

### ALTAR
Key references in Revelation: Rev 6:9; 8:3,5; 9:13; 11:1; 14:18; 16:7
- G2379 thysiastErion appears 8x in Revelation (22x total NT)
- Sacrifice altar (unqualified): Rev 6:9; 11:1
- Golden/incense altar (qualified chrysoun): Rev 8:3; 9:13
- Judgment/vindication context: Rev 14:18; 16:7

### GOLDEN ALTAR
Related to ALTAR. The golden altar of incense:
- Exo 30:1-10 — construction and function
- Exo 30:6 — positioned before the veil, before the mercy seat
- Rev 8:3 — golden altar before the throne
- Rev 9:13 — four horns of the golden altar before God

### TEMPLE / SANCTUARY
Key references: Rev 3:12; 7:15; 11:1,2,19; 14:15,17; 15:5,6,8; 16:1,17; 21:22
- G3485 naos (inner shrine) — 16x in Revelation, 0x hieron
- G2411 hieron (temple precincts) — ABSENT from Revelation entirely

### TABERNACLE
Key references: Rev 13:6; 15:5; 21:3
- G4633 skene — 3x in Revelation
- G4637 skenoO (to tabernacle/dwell) — 4x in Revelation (7:15; 12:12; 13:6; 21:3)

### INCENSE
Key references: Rev 5:8; 8:3,4; 18:13
- G2368 thymiama — 4x in Revelation (6x total NT)
- Explicitly identified as prayers of saints (Rev 5:8)

### LAMB OF GOD
Key references: 29x in Revelation using arnion (G721)
- Rev 5:6,8,12,13; 6:1,16; 7:9,10,14,17; 12:11; 13:8,11; 14:1,4,10; 15:3; 17:14; 19:7,9; 21:9,14,22,23,27; 22:1,3

### PRIEST
Key references: Rev 1:6; 5:10; 20:6
- Believers made kings and priests (hiereis)
- Priestly garments on Christ (1:13) and angels (15:6)

### WORSHIP
Pervasive throughout: Rev 4:10; 5:14; 7:11; 11:1,16; 14:7; 15:4; 19:4,10; 22:8,9
