# Topics Research

## Question
How do Ezekiel's sanctuary visions (defilement, departure of glory, future temple) and Zechariah's courtroom scene (chapter 3) illuminate sanctuary theology?

## Topics

### TEMPLE (Score: 0.50)
Key references: EZK 8:16; 10:3,4; 37:26,28; 42:20; 43:13-27
- Temple defilement from within by Israel's leaders
- Glory departure in stages (Ezek 9-11)
- Future temple vision (Ezek 40-48)
- Glory returns from the east (43:2-5)

### SANCTUARY (Score: 0.48)
Key references: HEB 9:2; EXO 25:8; LEV 19:30; EZK 11:16; HEB 8:2,5
- God as "little sanctuary" in exile (11:16)
- Eternal sanctuary promise (37:26-28)
- Heavenly sanctuary reality (Heb 8:2)

### PURIFICATION (Score: 0.48)
Key references: EZK 36:25; HEB 9:12-14,19; HEB 10:22
- Sprinkling clean water (36:25) — priestly purification vocabulary
- Red heifer ashes sprinkled (Num 19)
- Heavenly things purified with better sacrifices (Heb 9:23)

### ATONEMENT (Score: 0.53)
Key references: LEV 16:2-34; HEB 9:7,12,28; DAN 9:24-27; ZEC 13:1
- Day of Atonement ritual
- Zechariah 13:1 fountain for sin and uncleanness
- Daniel 9:24 finishing transgression

### JUDGMENT (Score: 0.48)
Key references: DAN 7:9,10; EZK 9:4-6; 18:20-28; REV 20:11-15
- Judgment begins at the sanctuary (Ezek 9:6)
- Marking/sealing before judgment
- Court convenes, books opened (Dan 7:9-10)

### PRIEST (Score: 0.46)
Key references: HEB 5:6,10; 7:1-21; ZEC 6:12-13; EXO 28:1-4
- Melchizedek priest-king typology
- The Branch as priest on his throne (Zec 6:13)
- Priestly garments and sin-bearing (Exo 28:38)

### RIVER (Score: 0.48)
Key references: PSA 36:8; 46:4; EZK 47:1-12; REV 22:1,2
- River from temple threshold (Ezek 47)
- River of water of life (Rev 22)
- Fountain from the house of the LORD (Joel 3:18)

### FALSE ACCUSATION (Score: 0.50)
Key references: EXO 23:1,7; PSA 41:5-9; JOB 1:9,10
- Satan as formal accuser (Job 1:6-12; Zec 3:1)
- Accusation before God day and night (Rev 12:10)

### REGENERATION (Score: 0.39)
Key references: EZK 11:19,20; 36:25-27; JHN 3:3-12; TIT 3:5,6
- New heart, new spirit (Ezek 36:26-27)
- Internal transformation, not just ritual

## Related Topics Discovered
- INTERCESSION: Christ's ongoing advocacy (Heb 7:25; Rom 8:34; 1 John 2:1)
- COVENANT: New covenant with internalized law (Jer 31:31-34; Heb 8:8-12)
- RESURRECTION: Dry bones vision (Ezek 37) connected to eschatological resurrection
