# Hebrew Parsing Results

## Ezekiel 9:4
Key terms: tav (mark) H8420, mitsach (forehead), anach (gasp/sigh Niphal), anaq (groan Niphal), toevah (abomination)
Command: hithvita tav - 'mark a mark' (Hiphil perfect 2ms) upon foreheads of those who sigh and cry

## Ezekiel 36:25
Key terms: zaraq (toss/sprinkle Qal perf 1s), mayim tehorim (pure waters), taher (be clean Qal perf 2mp / Piel impf 1s)
God sprinkles clean water -> they become clean -> He cleanses from all uncleanness and idols
