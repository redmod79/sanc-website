**Key Findings from Related Studies:**

**From ezekiel-sanctuary-defilement/CONCLUSION.md:**
- Four progressive abominations in Ezekiel 8: image of jealousy, animal worship by 70 elders, Tammuz worship by women, sun worship by 25 men between porch and altar
- Root cause: practical atheism - The LORD seeth us not (8:12; 9:9)
- Glory departs in 3 stages: (1) cherub to threshold (9:3; 10:4), (2) threshold to east gate (10:18-19), (3) city to Mount of Olives (11:22-23)
- Departure was RELUCTANT - each stage represents a delay, opportunity for repentance
- Judgment begins at the sanctuary (9:6) - echoes 1 Peter 4:17
- Restoration promises: cleansing (36:25), new heart/spirit (36:26-27), eternal sanctuary (37:26-27), glory returns from the east (43:2-5)
- Shared vocabulary with Daniel: shiqquts (H8251)

**From sanctuary-vindication-meaning/CONCLUSION.md:**
- Sanctuary vindication = heavenly court renders verdict answering Satans accusations
- Four things vindicated: Gods truth/law, Gods people, Gods plan, Gods character
- Zechariah 3 identified as perfect picture of sanctuary vindication
- Satan accuses, LORD rebukes, filthy garments removed, clean garments placed
- Pattern from Job: Satan accused Job, Job vindicated - same pattern for sanctuary
- Christ intercedes as defense attorney (Rom 8:34; Heb 7:25)
