**Key Observations:**
- ἱλαστήριον (G2435) = the same word used in LXX for the כַּפֹּרֶת (kapporeth, mercy seat)
- Only 2 NT occurrences: Rom 3:25 ("propitiation") and Heb 9:5 ("mercyseat")

- προέθετο (Middle voice) = "God publicly set forth [for himself]" -- God's deliberate act
- The connection: In Lev 16, the LORD's goat blood was sprinkled ON the mercy seat; in Rom 3:25, Christ IS the mercy seat AND the sacrifice
