## Leviticus 16:20 (Closure of Blood Atonement -- killah)

Hebrew Text: וְכִלָּה֙ מִכַּפֵּ֣ר אֶת־הַקֹּ֔דֶשׁ וְאֶת־אֹ֥הֶל מֹועֵ֖ד וְאֶת־הַמִּזְבֵּ֑חַ וְהִקְרִ֖יב אֶת־הַשָּׂעִ֥יר הֶחָֽי׃

**Key Observations:**
- Same verb שִׁלַּ֛ח (Piel of shalach = "send away") used for both the living bird (14:7) and the scapegoat (16:21,22)
- Living bird goes עַל־פְּנֵ֥י הַשָּׂדֶֽה ("upon the face of the open field") -- similar to scapegoat's הַמִּדְבָּֽרָה ("toward the wilderness")
- Both ceremonies involve: (1) one creature killed, (2) one creature released alive, (3) the living one sent away
- The living bird is dipped in the blood of the slaughtered bird -- it carries the blood of death but lives
