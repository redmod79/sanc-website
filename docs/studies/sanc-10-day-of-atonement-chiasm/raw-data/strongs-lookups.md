## H3725: כִּפֻּר (kippur) -- "atonement, expiation"
- **Transliteration:** kip-poor
- **POS:** masculine plural noun
- **BLB Count:** 8 occurrences
- **Definition:** From H3722; expiation (only in plural) -- atonement.
- **Occurrences:** Exo 29:36; 30:10; 30:16; Lev 23:27; 23:28; 25:9; Num 5:8; 29:11

- Does NOT appear in Lev 16 itself -- only in the parallel passages about the day
