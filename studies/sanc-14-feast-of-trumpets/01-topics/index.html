    Topics Research¶

    Question¶

    What is the Feast of Trumpets (Leviticus 23:24-25; Numbers 29:1-6) and how does it function as the herald of the Day of Atonement?

    Topics¶

    TRUMPET¶

    Full Nave's Entry: - Made of ram's horn: JOS 6:4-6,8,13 - Made of silver: NUM 10:2 - Uses of, prescribed by Moses: NUM 10:1-10 - Used in war: JOB 39:24,25; JER 4:19; 6:1,17; 42:14; 51:27; EZK 7:14; AMO 2:2; 3:6; ZEP 1:16; 1CO 14:8 - To summon soldiers: NUM 31:6; JDG 3:27; 6:34; 7:8-22; 1SA 13:3; 2SA 2:28; 15:10; 18:16; 20:1,22; NEH 4:18,20 - In war, of Abijah: 2CH 13:12,14 - In the siege of Jericho: JOS 6:4-20 - Sounded in time of danger: EZK 33:3-6; JOL 2:1 - Used at Mount Sinai: EXO 19:13-19; 20:18; HEB 12:19 - On the great day of atonement: ISA 27:13 - At the jubilee: LEV 25:9 - At the bringing up of the ark: 2SA 6:5,15; 1CH 13:8; 15:28 - At the anointing of kings: 1KI 1:34,39; 2KI 9:13; 11:14 - At the dedication of Solomon's temple: 2CH 5:12,13; 7:6 - In worship: 1CH 15:24; 16:42; 25:5; PSA 81:3,4 - At Jehoshaphat's triumph: 2CH 20:28 - At the foundation of the second temple: EZR 3:10,11 - At the dedication of the wall: NEH 12:35,41 - FIGURATIVE: ISA 27:13; EZK 33:3; JOL 2:1; ZEC 9:14; MAT 6:2 - SYMBOLICAL: MAT 24:31; 1CO 15:52; 1TH 4:16; REV 1:10; 4:1; 8; 9:1-14; 10:7; 11:15

    ETHANIM (The Seventh Month -- Feast Calendar Context)¶

    • The seventh month (October/Tishri)
    • Feast of Trumpets in: LEV 23:23-25
    • Day of Atonement, on the tenth day of: LEV 23:26-32
    • Feast of Tabernacles in: LEV 23:33-43
    • Jubilee proclaimed on the tenth day of: LEV 25:9
    • Temple dedicated in: 1KI 8:2
    • Altar restored in, after the captivity: EZR 3:1,6

    SELF-EXAMINATION¶

    • General scriptures concerning: JOB 13:23; PSA 4:4; 19:12; 26:2; 77:6; 119:59; 139:23,24; JER 17:9; LAM 3:40; HAG 1:7; MAT 26:22; MRK 14:19; 1CO 11:27,28,31; 2CO 13:5; GAL 6:3-5
    • See MEDITATION, REPENTANCE, SIN/CONFESSION OF

    WATCHFULNESS¶

    • General scriptures concerning: EXO 23:13; 34:12; DEU 4:9,23; 6:17; 11:16; 12:13; 27:9; JOS 22:5; 23:11; 1KI 2:3,4; 8:25; 2CH 19:7; NEH 4:9; JOB 36:18-21; PSA 39:1; 102:7; 119:9; 141:3; PRO 4:23,25,26; 8:34; 16:17; 28:26; NAM 2:1; HAB 2:1; MAL 2:15; MAT 6:1; 16:6; 18:10; 24:4; 25:13; 24:42-51; 26:40,41; MRK 4:24; 13:9-23,32-37; LUK 8:18; 11:35; 12:15,35-40; 21:8-36; ACT 20:28-31; ROM 11:21; 13:11; 1CO 7:29-31; 9:27; 10:12; 11:28; 16:13; GAL 6:1; EPH 5:15; 6:18; PHP 3:2; 4:5; COL 4:2,17; 1TH 5:4,6,21; 1TI 4:16; 2TI 4:5; HEB 2:1; 3:12; 12:15; 1PE 1:13,17; 4:7; 5:8; 2PE 1:19; 3:17; 1JN 5:18; 2JN 1:8; Jude 1:20,21; REV 3:2,3,11; 16:15

    REPENTANCE¶

    • (A complete reversal of one's attitude and values, i.e. a turning toward God)
    • Attributed to God: GEN 6:6,7; EXO 32:14; DEU 32:36; JDG 2:18; JOL 2:13; JON 3:9,10
    • The burden of the preaching of John the Baptist (MAT 3) and Jesus (MAT 4:17; MRK 1:15)
    • Exhortation to: PRO 1:23-33; JER 7:3,5; 26:3; HOS 14:1-3; AMO 5:4-6; MAT 3:2
    • Condition of God's favor: LEV 26:40-42; 2CH 7:14
    • Key trumpet-related texts: JOL 1:14; 2:12,13,15-18 (trumpet call to repentance); AMO 4:12 ("prepare to meet thy God"); REV 2:5; 3:2,3,19 (warnings to churches)
    • Instance relevant to Feast of Trumpets: "After hearing the law expounded by Ezra NEH 8:1-12; 9:1-3"

    MEMORIAL¶

    • The Passover: EXO 12:14
    • Firstborn set apart as a: EXO 13:12-16
    • The pot of manna: EXO 16:32-34
    • The Feast of Tabernacles: LEV 23:43
    • Shoulder stones of the ephod: EXO 28:12
    • The Lord's Supper: LUK 22:19; 1CO 11:24-26

    JUDGMENT (THE GENERAL)¶

    • Key scriptures: 1CH 16:33; PSA 9:7; 50:3-6; 96:13; 98:9; ECC 3:17; 11:9; 12:14; DAN 7:9,10; AMO 4:12; MAT 25:1-46; JHN 5:22; 12:48; ACT 17:31; ROM 2:5-10; 14:10-12; 2CO 5:10; 2TH 1:7,8; HEB 6:2; 9:27; REV 1:7; 6:15-17; 11:18; 20:11-15; 22:12
    • According to opportunity and works: [extensive list including EZK 18:4-9,19-32; 33:18-20]

    Related Topics Discovered¶

    ETHANIM¶

    Cross-reference topic showing the entire seventh-month festival sequence: Trumpets (1st) -> Atonement (10th) -> Tabernacles (15th-22nd). This structural relationship is the backbone of the study.

    SEVEN (symbolic use)¶

    From the SEVEN topic in Nave's: "Seven months: Holy convocations in the seventh month LEV 23:24-44; NUM 29; EZK 45:25" and "Seven angels with seven trumpets REV 8:2" -- connecting the liturgical sevens with the apocalyptic sevens.
