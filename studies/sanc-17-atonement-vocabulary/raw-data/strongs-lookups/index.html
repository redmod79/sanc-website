    H3724 kopher (noun-m, 17 BLB occurrences)¶

    Definition: from [H3722]; properly, a cover, i.e. (literally) a village (as covered in); (specifically) bitumen (as used for coating), and the henna plant (as used for dyeing); figuratively, a redemption-price: bribe, camphire, pitch, ransom, satisfaction, sum of money, village

    Translation Distribution (16 occurrences, 12 unique):¶

    • 3 (18.8%) "a ransom"
    • 2 (12.5%) "satisfaction"
    • 2 (12.5%) "ransom"
    • 1 each: "with pitch", "a sum of money", "villages", "bribe", "a ransom for him", "The ransom", "thy ransom", "a bribe"

    Biblical Usage: Genesis 6:14; Exodus 21:30(x2); 30:12; Numbers 35:31,32; 1 Samuel 6:18; 12:3; Job 33:24; 36:18; Psalms 49:8(x2); Proverbs 6:35; 13:8; 21:18; Song of Songs 1:14; 4:13; Isaiah 43:3(x2); Amos 5:12¶

    Key verses for "ransom": Job 36:18; Proverbs 6:35¶

    Key verses for "satisfaction": Numbers 35:31,32¶

    LXX Translation:¶

    • G3083 lytron: PMI 8.83, count 6 (key connection: kopher -> lytron = ransom price)

    H3680 kasah (verb, 152 BLB occurrences)¶

    Definition: a primitive root; properly, to plump, i.e. fill up hollows; by implication, to cover (for clothing or secrecy): clad self, close, clothe, conceal, cover (self), (flee to) hide, overwhelm Note: Parallel covering concept to kaphar. Kasah = physical covering; kaphar = spiritual/ritual covering.

    Translation Distribution (2 occurrences):¶

    • 1 "a propitiation" (ROM 3:25)
    • 1 "mercyseat" (HEB 9:5)

    LXX Usage: Exodus 25:17,18; 37:6; 38:7; Leviticus 16:2 (always = kapporeth/mercy seat)¶

    Hebrew Sources:¶

    • H5715 eduth: PMI 6.66, count 5 (testimony)
    • H727 aron: PMI 5.31, count 6 (ark)

    G2433 hilaskomai (verb, 2 NT occurrences)¶

    Translation Distribution (2 occurrences):¶

    • 1 "be merciful" (LUK 18:13)
    • 1 "make reconciliation for" (HEB 2:17)

    LXX Usage: Exodus 32:14; 2 Kings 5:18; Psalms 25:11; 78:9; 79:9; Proverbs 16:14¶

    Hebrew Sources:¶

    • H5545 salach: PMI 8.10, count 6 (primary: forgive/pardon)
    • H3722 kaphar: PMI 6.47, count 4 (core connection: atone)

    G2434 hilasmos (noun, NOT in lexicon DB but found in Greek text)¶

    G2643 katallage (noun-f, 4 BLB occurrences)¶

    Biblical Usage: Romans 5:11; 11:15; 2 Corinthians 5:18,19(?)¶

    Note: NT lexicon lists 3 verses but 4 occurrences. Parser confirms: ROM 5:11 (katallagen), 2CO 5:18 (katallages), 2CO 5:19 (katallages).

    G604 apokatallasso (verb, 3 BLB occurrences)¶

    Translation Distribution (3 occurrences):¶

    • 1 "to reconcile" (COL 1:20)
    • 1 "hath he reconciled" (COL 1:21)

    G3083 lytron (noun, 2 NT occurrences)¶

    Definition: from [G3089]; something to loosen with, i.e. a redemption price

    Translation Distribution (2 occurrences):¶

    LXX Usage: Exodus 21:30; Leviticus 19:20; Numbers 35:31; Isaiah 45:13¶

    G487 antilytron (noun, 1 NT occurrence)¶
