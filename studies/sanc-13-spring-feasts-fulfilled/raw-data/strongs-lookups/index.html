    Raw Strong's Lookup Output¶

    G3957 pascha¶

    • 19 KJV occurrences, 3 unique translations: passover (17), the Passover (1), Easter (1)
    • BLB count: 29
    • Lexicon: πάσχα (páscha), neuter noun; of Chaldee origin; the Passover (the meal, the day, the festival or the victim)
    • Verse references: Mat 26:2,17,18,19; Mar 14:1,12,12,14,16; Luk 2:41; 22:1,7,8,11,13,15; Jhn 2:13,2:23,6:4,11:55,12:1,13:1,18:28,18:39,19:14; Act 12:4; 1Co 5:7; Heb 11:28
    • LXX from H6453: 27 mappings (PMI 8.89); also maps to G2380 thyo (9 occurrences)

    G286 amnos¶

    • 2 KJV occurrences, 2 translations: a lamb (1), of a lamb (1)
    • BLB count: 4
    • Lexicon: ἀμνός (amnós), masculine noun; a lamb
    • Verses: Jhn 1:29, 1:36, Act 8:32, 1Pe 1:19

    G721 arnion¶

    • 29 KJV occurrences, 4 translations: Lamb (24), a Lamb (2), Lamb's (2), a lamb (1)
    • BLB count: 30
    • Lexicon: ἀρνίον (arníon), neuter noun; diminutive form; a lambkin
    • Primary usage: Revelation (29x), John 21:15 (1x)

    G536 aparche¶

    • 7 KJV occurrences, 3 translations: the firstfruits (5), firstfruits (1), firstfruit (1)
    • BLB count: 8
    • Lexicon: ἀπαρχή (aparchē), feminine noun; a beginning of sacrifice, the first portion
    • Verses traced: Rom 8:23, 11:16, 16:5; 1Co 15:20, 15:23, 16:15; 2Th 2:13; Jas 1:18; Rev 14:4

    H4150 mo'ed¶

    • 229 KJV occurrences, 68 unique translations
    • Top: of the congregation (146), the feasts (4), appointed (3), in his appointed season (3)
    • BLB count: 223
    • Lexicon: מוֹעֵד (mo'ed), masculine noun; an appointment, fixed time/season, festival, assembly, congregation, meeting place
    • LXX mappings: G3142 martyrion (138), G4633 skene (138), G1859 heorte (34), G2540 kairos (29), G3831 panegyris (3)

    H6453 pesach¶

    • 50 KJV occurrences, 9 translations: the passover (32), passover (6), of the passover (4)
    • BLB count: 49
    • Lexicon: פֶּסַח (pesach), masculine noun; from pasach (to pass/leap over); pretermission, exemption; the Jewish Passover
    • Distribution: Exo 12 (15x), Num 9 (12x), Deu 16 (7x), 2Ch 35 (10x), other
    • LXX: G3957 pascha (27 mappings, PMI 8.89)

    G3904 paraskeue¶

    • 3 KJV occurrences, 2 translations: the preparation (2), day of the preparation (1)
    • BLB count: 6
    • Lexicon: παρασκευή (paraskeuē), feminine noun; readiness, preparation
    • All NT: Mat 27:62, Mar 15:42, Luk 23:54, Jhn 19:14, 19:31, 19:42

    G1859 heorte¶

    • 10 KJV occurrences, 3 translations: feast (8), the feast (1), of an holyday (1)
    • BLB count: 27
    • Lexicon: ἑορτή (heortē), feminine noun; a festival
    • Key: Col 2:16 ("holyday"), multiple Johannine feast references

    H1061 bikkuwr¶

    • 18 KJV occurrences, 9 translations: of the firstfruits (6), the firstfruits (4)
    • BLB count: 18
    • Lexicon: בִּכּוּר (bikkuwr), masculine plural noun; first-fruits of the crop
    • Key: Lev 23:17,20 (Pentecost loaves), Num 28:26 (day of firstfruits)
    • LXX: G4374 prosphero (5, PMI 5.86)

    H4682 matstsah¶

    • 60 KJV occurrences, 14 translations: unleavened bread (15), of unleavened bread (14), unleavened (12)
    • BLB count: 53
    • Lexicon: מַצָּה (matstsah), feminine noun; sweetness, unfermented cake/loaf, the festival of Passover
    • Heavy concentration in Exodus 12, Leviticus feast legislation

    H2282 chag¶

    • 69 KJV occurrences, 28 translations: a feast (12), the feast (12), feast (10)
    • BLB count: 62
    • Lexicon: חַג (chag), masculine noun; festival, or victim therefor; solemn feast, sacrifice, solemnity
    • From chagag (to celebrate/keep a feast); designates pilgrimage feasts

    LXX Mappings¶

    H6453 (pesach) -> Greek¶

    • G3957 pascha: 27 (PMI 8.89) -- primary equivalent
    • G2380 thyo: 9 (PMI 5.35) -- sacrificial dimension
    • G106 azymos: 3 (PMI 5.14) -- unleavened connection
    • G1859 heorte: 3 (PMI 4.16) -- feast connection

    H4150 (mo'ed) -> Greek¶

    • G3142 martyrion: 138 -- "tent of meeting" usage
    • G4633 skene: 138 -- "tabernacle" usage
    • G1859 heorte: 34 -- "feast" usage
    • G2540 kairos: 29 -- "appointed time" usage
    • G3831 panegyris: 3 (PMI 6.33) -- "general assembly" (Heb 12:23)

    H1061 (bikkuwr) -> Greek¶

    • G4374 prosphero: 5 (PMI 5.86) -- "to bring/offer"
    • G740 artos: 3 (PMI 4.14) -- "bread" (the loaves connection)
