    Related Existing Studies¶

    sanc-14-feast-of-trumpets¶

    Question: What is the Feast of Trumpets and how does it herald the Day of Atonement? Key findings (from PROMPT.md summary): - Feast of Trumpets (Tishri 1) is the herald of the DOA (Tishri 10); nine-day interval = window for self-examination - Three-level trumpet theology: (1) Feast of Trumpets = herald/alarm/warning, (2) DOA = judgment/cleansing, (3) Jubilee trumpet on DOA = liberation/restoration/return to inheritance - yobel (H3104) connects Jericho trumpets (Josh 6:4-5) to Jubilee trumpet (Lev 25:9-10) — conquest and liberation share same instrument - Seventh trumpet of Revelation (Rev 11:15-18) simultaneously announces judgment AND reward - teruah (H8643) spans alarm + acclamation + battle cry - LXX bridges teruah/shophar to salpinx (G4536)

    day-of-atonement-revelation-chiasm¶

    Question: How does the DOA align with Revelation's chiastic structure? Key findings: - Revelation's chiastic structure (A-B-C-D-E-D'-C'-B'-A') follows Yom Kippur liturgical sequence - Rev 11:19 (ark revealed) = structural pivot = high priest enters Most Holy Place - Rev 15:8 "no man able to enter" parallels Lev 16:17 "no man in the tabernacle" - Scapegoat (Lev 16:20-22) antityped by Satan bound in abyss (Rev 20:1-3) - Goal: restoration of direct divine presence — Lev 16:30 = Rev 21:3 - Jubilee trumpet sounded ON Day of Atonement — 7th trumpet = "The kingdoms of this world are become the kingdoms of our Lord" - Feast calendar mapped: Trumpets (Rev 8-11) → DOA (Rev 15-16) → Tabernacles (Rev 21-22)

    sanc-09-day-of-atonement-ritual¶

    Question: What is the exact DOA ritual sequence (Lev 16)? Key findings: - DOA primarily cleanses SANCTUARY, not people directly - Two irreducible phases: blood atonement (LORD's goat) + sin removal (scapegoat) - kol-adam exclusion (Lev 16:17) = structural center; atonement exclusively God's work through mediator - Three sin categories: tum'ah, pesha, chattat — full spectrum - Lev 16:30 purpose: "that ye may be clean from ALL your sins before the LORD" - kaphar (H3722) appears 16 times in Lev 16 - Heb 9:12 — Christ enters heavenly sanctuary having obtained ETERNAL redemption (aionian lutrosin) - Heb 9:15 — "they which are called might receive the promise of ETERNAL INHERITANCE"
