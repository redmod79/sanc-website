    Topics Research¶

    Question¶

    Leviticus 25:9 commands the Jubilee trumpet to sound ON the Day of Atonement. What is the Jubilee, and what does it mean that liberty is proclaimed specifically when judgment occurs?

    Topics¶

    JUBILEE¶

    • Called THE ACCEPTABLE YEAR OF THE LORD ISA 61:2
    • Called THE YEAR OF LIBERTY EZK 46:17
    • LAWS CONCERNING LEV 25:8-55; 27:17-24; NUM 36:4

    LIBERTY¶

    • Proclaimed in the year of jubilee LEV 25:10; JER 34:8,15-17
    • Political JDG 17:6; 21:25; ACT 22:28
    • See JUBILEE
    • FIGURATIVE ISA 61:2; 63:4; LUK 4:19; JHN 8:32,33,36; GAL 3:28; EPH 6:8; COL 3:11

    REDEMPTION¶

    • Of persons or property LEV 25:25-34; 27:2-13,14-33; RUT 4:3-10
    • Redemption money paid to priests NUM 3:46-51
    • Of the firstborn — See FIRSTBORN
    • Of land — See JUBILEE
    • OF OUR SOULS PSA 111:9; 130:7; MAT 20:28; MRK 10:45; LUK 2:38; ACT 20:28; ROM 3:24-26; 1CO 1:30; 6:20; 7:23; GAL 1:4; 2:20; 4:4,5; EPH 1:7; 5:2; COL 1:14,20-22; 1TI 2:6; TIT 2:14; HEB 9:12,15; 1PE 1:18,19; REV 5:9,10
    • See ATONEMENT
    • See RANSOM

    ATONEMENT¶

    • For tabernacle and furniture LEV 16:15-20,33
    • In consummation of the Levites NUM 8:21
    • For those defiled by the dead NUM 6:11
    • Made for houses LEV 14:53
    • For sin, see below
    • By meat offerings LEV 5:11-13
    • By jewels NUM 31:50
    • By money EXO 30:12-16; LEV 5:15,16; 2KI 12:16
    • By incense NUM 16:46-50
    • By animals, see below
    • By Jesus, see below
    • DAY OF
    • Time of EXO 30:10; LEV 23:27; 25:9; NUM 29:7
    • How observed EXO 30:10; LEV 16:2-34; 23:27-32; NUM 29:7-11; ACT 27:9; HEB 5:3; 9:7,19,22
    • MADE BY ANIMAL SACRIFICES EXO 29:36; 30:12-16; LEV 1:4; 4:20,22-35; 5:6-10; 6:7; 9:7; 10:17; 12:6-8; 14:12-32; 16:6,10,11,15-19,24-34; 17:11; 19:22; NUM 15:22-28; 28:22,30; 29:5,10,11; HEB 9:22
    • MADE BY JESUS
    • Divinely ordained LUK 2:30,31; GAL 4:4,5; EPH 1:3-12,17-22; 2:4-10; COL 1:19,20; 1PE 1:20; REV 13:8
    • A mystery 1CO 2:7
    • With context 1PE 1:8-12
    • Made but once HEB 7:27; 9:24-28; 10:10,12,14; 1PE 3:18
    • Redemption by MAT 20:28; ACT 20:28; GAL 3:13; 1TI 2:6; HEB 9:12; REV 5:9
    • Typified GEN 4:4; with HEB 11:4; GEN 22:2; with HEB 11:17,19; EXO 12:5,11,14; with 1CO 5:7; EXO 24:8; with HEB 9:20; LEV 16:30,34; with HEB 9:7,12,28; LEV 17:11; with HEB 9:22
    • See SALVATION, PLAN OF
    • UNCLASSIFIED SCRIPTURES RELATING TO PSA 40:6,7; ISA 53:4-12; DAN 9:24-27; ZEC 13:1; MAT 26:28; LUK 22:20; 24:46,47; JHN 1:29,36; 6:51; 11:49-51; ACT 17:2,3; 20:28; ROM 3:24-26; 4:25; 5:1,2,6-11,15-21; 1CO 1:17,18,23,24; 15:3; 2CO 5:18,19; GAL 1:3,4; 4:4,5; EPH 1:7; 2:13-18; 5:2,25; COL 1:14,19-22; 1TH 5:9,10; 1TI 2:5,6; TIT 2:14; HEB 1:3; 2:9,17; 9:12-15,25,26; 10:1-20; 12:24; 13:12,20,21; 1PE 1:18-20; 2:24; 3:18; 1JN 1:7; 2:2; 3:5; 4:10; 5:6; REV 1:5; 5:9; 7:14; 12:11

    INHERITANCE¶

    • Provisions for inheritance under Levirate marriages GEN 38:7-11; NUM 36:6-9; DEU 25:5-10; RUT 3:1-8; 4:7-17
    • UNCLASSIFIED SCRIPTURES RELATING TO GEN 15:3; 21:9-11; 24:36; 25:5,6; 48:21,22; NUM 27:6-11; DEU 21:15-17; 1KI 21:3; 2CH 21:3; JOB 42:15; PRO 17:2; 20:21; ECC 2:18,19; JER 32:6-8; EZK 46:16-18; LUK 15:12,25-31; GAL 3:15; HEB 9:16,17
    • See WILL
    • See HEIR
    • FIGURATIVE PSA 37:29; ACT 20:32; 26:18; ROM 8:16,17; EPH 1:11-14; TIT 3:7; HEB 1:14
    • See FIRSTBORN
    • See HEIR
    • See WILL

    HEIR¶

    • General scriptures concerning GAL 4:1,2; GEN 15:3; 21:10; 25:5,6; LEV 25:45,46; NUM 27:8-11; 36:1-8; DEU 21:15-17; JOS 17:3-6; RUT 4:1-12; ECC 2:18,19; GAL 4:30
    • See INHERITANCE, BIRTHRIGHT, FIRSTBORN, ORPHAN, WILL
    • FIGURATIVE ROM 8:14-17; GAL 3:29; 4:6,7; TIT 3:7; JAS 2:5
    • See ADOPTION

    CAPTIVITY¶

    • Of the Israelites foretold LEV 26:33; DEU 28:36
    • Of the ten tribes 2KI 17:6,23,24; 18:9-12
    • Of Judah in Babylon, prophecy of ISA 39:6; JER 13:19; 20:4; 25:2-11; 32:28
    • Fulfilled 2KI 24:11-16; 25; 2CH 36; JER 52:28-30
    • Jews return from EZR 2; 3; 8
    • Israelites in, promises to NEH 1:9
    • As a judgment EZR 5:12; 9:7; ISA 5:13; JER 29:17-19; LAM 1:3-5; EZK 39:23,24
    • FIGURATIVE ISA 61:1; ROM 7:23; 1CO 9:27; 2CO 10:5; 2TI 2:26; 3:6
    • Captivity led captive JDG 5:12; PSA 68:18; EPH 4:8

    BONDAGE¶

    • Of Israelites in Egypt EXO 1:14; 2:23; 6:6
    • In Persia EZR 9:9
    • See EMANCIPATION
    • See SERVANT

    RESTORATION¶

    • Of the Jews — See ISRAELITES
    • Of all things ACT 3:21; REV 21:1-5

    EMANCIPATION¶

    • Of all Jewish servants at the time of jubilee LEV 25:8-17
    • Proclamation of
    • By Zedekiah JER 34:8-11
    • By Cyrus 2CH 36:23; EZR 1:1-4
    • See EXODUS
    • See JUBILEE

    TRUMPET¶

    • Made of ram's horn JOS 6:4-6,8,13
    • Made of silver NUM 10:2
    • Uses of, prescribed by Moses NUM 10:1-10
    • Used in war JOB 39:24,25; JER 4:19; 6:1,17; 42:14; 51:27; EZK 7:14; AMO 2:2; 3:6; ZEP 1:16; 1CO 14:8
    • To summon soldiers (Phinehas NUM 31:6; Ehud JDG 3:27; Gideon JDG 6:34; Saul 1SA 13:3; Joab 2SA 2:28; 18:16; 20:22; Absalom 2SA 15:10; Sheba 2SA 20:1; Nehemiah NEH 4:18,20)
    • By Gideon's soldiers JDG 7:8-22
    • In war, of Abijah 2CH 13:12,14
    • In the siege of Jericho JOS 6:4-20
    • Sounded in time of danger EZK 33:3-6; JOL 2:1
    • Used at Mount Sinai EXO 19:13-19; 20:18; HEB 12:19
    • On the great day of atonement ISA 27:13
    • At the jubilee LEV 25:9
    • At the bringing up of the ark 2SA 6:5,15; 1CH 13:8; 15:28
    • At the anointing of kings 1KI 1:34,39; 2KI 9:13; 11:14
    • At the dedication of Solomon's temple 2CH 5:12,13; 7:6
    • In worship 1CH 15:24; 16:42; 25:5; PSA 81:3,4
    • At Jehoshaphat's triumph 2CH 20:28
    • At the foundation of the second temple EZR 3:10,11
    • At the dedication of the wall NEH 12:35,41
    • FIGURATIVE ISA 27:13; EZK 33:3; JOL 2:1; ZEC 9:14; MAT 6:2
    • SYMBOLICAL MAT 24:31; 1CO 15:52; 1TH 4:16; REV 1:10; 4:1; 8; 9:1-14; 10:7; 11:15

    ETHANIM¶

    • (The seventh month, October)
    • Feast of Trumpets in LEV 23:23-25
    • Day of Atonement, on the tenth day of LEV 23:26-32
    • Feast of Tabernacles in LEV 23:33-43
    • Jubilee proclaimed on the tenth day of LEV 25:9
    • Temple dedicated in 1KI 8:2
    • Altar restored in, after the captivity EZR 3:1,6

    Related Topics Discovered¶

    • SABBATIC YEAR (EXO 23:9-11; LEV 25; EXO 21:2; DEU 15:12; JER 34:14) — the seven-year cycle underlying the Jubilee
    • ANOINTING (ISA 61:1; LUK 4:18; DAN 9:24; ACT 4:27) — the anointed one who proclaims liberty
    • RANSOM — see REDEMPTION
    • SERVANT, BOND — see SLAVERY, EMANCIPATION
