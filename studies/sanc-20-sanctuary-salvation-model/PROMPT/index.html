    Bible Study: The Sanctuary as Salvation Model¶

    Question¶

    How does the sanctuary's architecture and ritual system teach the complete plan of salvation? Trace the sinner's journey from the gate through every compartment to God's presence, and show how each step corresponds to a phase of the salvation experience. Also show how the sanctuary teaches the prophetic timeline of salvation history from the cross to the new creation.

    Context¶

    This is the capstone study of the sanctuary series (sanc-01 through sanc-19). It SYNTHESIZES findings from all prior studies into a unified model that demonstrates the sanctuary as God's master illustration of salvation -- both as an individual experience and as a prophetic timeline.

    IMPORTANT: No EGW sources. Scripture only.

    Key Findings from Related Studies:
