    Raw Strong's Lookup Output¶

    H6607 -- pethach¶

    • Transliteration: pethach
    • Part of Speech: masculine noun (n-m)
    • BLB Count: 163
    • Definition: from patach; an opening (literally), i.e. door (gate) or entrance way -- door, entering (in), entrance (-ry), gate, opening, place.
    • 173 total occurrences in Strong's DB, 53 unique translations
    • Top: "the door" 58x (33.5%), "at the door" 15x (8.7%), "door" 15x (8.7%)
    • Key refs: Gen 4:7; 18:1; 19:6,11; Exo 29:4; 33:8-10; 35:15; 40:5,28,29; Lev 1:3; Num 3:25-26; Psa 24:7,9

    G2374 -- thyra¶

    • Transliteration: thyra
    • Part of Speech: feminine noun (n-f)
    • BLB Count: 39
    • Definition: apparently a primary word; a portal or entrance
    • 31 total occurrences, 6 unique translations
    • Top: "door" 19x (61.3%), "doors" 5x (16.1%), "a door" 3x (9.7%)
    • Key refs: John 10:1,7,9; 18:16; 20:19,26; Acts 3:2; 5:9,19,23; 12:6,13; 14:27; 16:26; 1 Cor 16:9; 2 Cor 2:12; Col 4:3; Jas 5:9; Rev 3:7,8,20; 4:1

    H4196 -- mizbeach¶

    • Transliteration: mizbeach
    • Part of Speech: masculine noun (n-m)
    • BLB Count: 402
    • Definition: from zabach (H2076); an altar
    • 412 total occurrences, 36 unique translations
    • Top: "the altar" 142x (34.5%), "of the altar" 59x (14.3%), "an altar" 46x (11.2%)
    • Etymology: from zabach = "to slaughter" -> mizbeach = "place of slaughter"

    G3824 -- palingenesia¶

    • Transliteration: palingenesia
    • Part of Speech: feminine noun (n-f)
    • BLB Count: 2
    • Definition: from palin (again) + genesis (birth); spiritual rebirth
    • Translations: "regeneration" 1x (Mat 19:28), "of regeneration" 1x (Tit 3:5)

    G3067 -- loutron¶

    • Transliteration: loutron
    • Part of Speech: neuter noun (n)
    • BLB Count: 2
    • Definition: from louo; a bath, i.e. (figuratively) baptism -- washing
    • Translations: "washing" 1x (Eph 5:26), "the washing" 1x (Tit 3:5)

    G3068 -- louo¶

    • Transliteration: louo
    • Part of Speech: verb (v)
    • BLB Count: 6
    • Definition: a primary verb; to bathe (the whole person; whereas nipto means to wet a part only)
    • Refs: Song 5:12; John 13:10; Acts 9:37; 16:33; Heb 10:22,23; 2 Pet 2:22; Rev 1:5

    G3538 -- nipto¶

    • Transliteration: nipto
    • Part of Speech: verb (v)
    • BLB Count: 17
    • Definition: to cleanse (especially the hands or the feet or the face)
    • Refs: Mat 6:17; 15:2; Mrk 7:3; John 9:7,11,15; 13:8,10; 1 Tim 5:10

    H3727 -- kapporeth¶

    • Transliteration: kapporeth
    • Part of Speech: feminine noun (n-f)
    • BLB Count: 27
    • Definition: from kaphar; a lid (used only of the cover of the sacred Ark) -- mercy seat
    • Translations: "the mercy seat" 22x, "of the mercy seat" 3x, "a mercy seat" 1x, "the mercy seatward" 1x
    • All 27 occurrences: Exo 25:17,18,19,20,21,22; 26:34; 30:6; 31:7; 35:12; 37:6,7,8,9; 39:35; 40:20; Lev 16:2,13,14,15; Num 7:89; 1 Chr 28:11
    • LXX: kapporeth -> hilasterion (G2435), PMI 9.75 (16x)

    H3722 -- kaphar¶

    • Transliteration: kaphar
    • Part of Speech: verb (v)
    • BLB Count: 102
    • Definition: a primitive root; to cover (specifically with bitumen); figuratively, to expiate or condone, to placate or cancel
    • 130 total occurrences, 51 unique translations
    • Top: "shall make an atonement" 20x, "to make an atonement" 18x, "and make an atonement" 10x
    • Leviticus 16 has 16 occurrences in single chapter
    • LXX: top mappings include peri (62x), hamartia (41x), aphiemi (14x), katharizo (14x)

    G2643 -- katallage¶

    • Transliteration: katallage
    • Part of Speech: feminine noun (n-f)
    • BLB Count: 4
    • Definition: from katallasso; exchange, i.e. restoration to divine favor -- atonement, reconciliation
    • All 4: Rom 5:11 ("atonement"), Rom 11:15 ("the reconciling"), 2 Cor 5:18 ("of reconciliation"), 2 Cor 5:19 ("of reconciliation")

    G4637 -- skenoo¶

    • Transliteration: skenoo
    • Part of Speech: verb (v)
    • BLB Count: 5
    • Definition: from skene (a tent); to tent or encamp, figuratively to occupy or reside (as God did in the Tabernacle)
    • All occurrences: Jhn 1:14 ("dwelt"), Rev 7:15 ("shall dwell"), Rev 12:12 ("ye that dwell"), Rev 13:6 ("dwell"), Rev 21:3 ("he will dwell")

    G1529 -- eisodos¶

    • Transliteration: eisodos
    • Part of Speech: feminine noun (n-f)
    • BLB Count: 5
    • Definition: from eis + hodos; an entrance (literally or figuratively)
    • All occurrences: Acts 13:24 ("coming"), 1 Thess 1:9 ("of entering in"), 1 Thess 2:1 ("entrance in"), Heb 10:19 ("enter"), 2 Pet 1:11 ("entrance")
