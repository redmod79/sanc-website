    Bible Study: Grand Synthesis -- The Sanctuary as God Answer¶

    Question¶

    Drawing on ALL previous sanctuary series studies (sanc-01 through sanc-29) and the christ-sin-bearer-as-high-priest study, synthesize: What is the sanctuary? Why does it matter? How does it illuminate salvation, prophecy, Revelation, and God character? What confidence can future studies place in the sanctuary framework?

    Key Findings from Related Studies¶

    sanc-01: Eden = proto-sanctuary. Miqdash + mishkan frame holiness problem. Glory-filling pattern ratifies each stage.

    sanc-11: Two goats = single chattat. LORD goat = kaphar. Scapegoat = removal. Lev 16:20a = structural closure.

    sanc-20: Sanctuary = salvation model: gate-altar-laver-HP-veil-MHP = faith-justification-regeneration-sanctification-mediation-glorification.

    sanc-25: nitsdaq = hapax Niphal of tsadaq. Forensic vindication, not ritual cleansing. kaphar produces tsadaq.

    sanc-28: 14+ Greek sanctuary terms, 70+ verses. naos 16x, hieron 0x. Vessel transformation. Altar vindication arc.

    sanc-29: Compartmental progression HP-MHP-no temple. DOA maps 7/8 steps. GAP: blood at mercy seat.

    christ-sin-bearer: nasa/anaphero = priestly function. Two vocabularies (sacrifice vs priest), one Christ. Scapegoat = Satan.

    Focus Areas¶

    1. Sanctuary as Visual Curriculum: Trace dwelling-purpose Eden to New Jerusalem. Retrieve EXO 25:8; HEB 9:8; 8:5; COL 2:17; JHN 1:14; REV 21:3,22.

    2. Sanctuary as Prophetic Framework: Map Dan 7:9-14, Dan 8:14 nitsdaq, Dan 9:24, Revelation progression. Show vocabulary-level connection.

    3. Sanctuary as Theodicy: tsadaq vindication framework. Altar arc Rev 6:10 to 16:7. True and righteous declarations.

    4. Sanctuary as Eschatology: HP intercession to MHP judgment to no-temple communion. Feast fulfillment timeline.

    5. Sanctuary as Christology: Complete map: gate-altar-laver-lampstand-showbread-incense-veil-ark-mercy seat-HIGH PRIEST-sacrifice-scapegoat binder.

    6. Priestly Sin-Bearing Integration: Two-vocabulary framework. Exo 28:38 to Lev 10:17 to Isa 53:12 to Heb 9:28 to 1 Pet 2:24.

    7. Evidence Tier Assessment: HIGH/MODERATE/OPEN for each major claim. Genuine gaps identified.

    8. Continuing Relevance: What the framework provides for future studies.

    Research Instructions¶

    Research agent: Retrieve all verses listed above with full chapter context for priority passages (Exodus 25, Leviticus 16, Hebrews 9, Revelation 21). Run cross-testament parallels for EXO 25:8, HEB 9:28, REV 21:22, ROM 3:25, DAN 8:14. Run parsing for EXO 25:8, HEB 9:28, REV 21:22, DAN 8:14. Complete word studies for H4720, G3485, G2435, H5375, G399, H3722, H6663.
